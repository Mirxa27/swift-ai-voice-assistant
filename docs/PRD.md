# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Newomen

Newomen is an AI companion designed to support women's mental health through culturally sensitive conversations and guided self‑reflection. The application focuses on personal growth journeys such as Narrative Identity Exploration while ensuring privacy and safety.

## Goals
- Offer a safe space for emotional support
- Encourage frequent engagement and self‑reflection
- Provide localized experiences in English and Arabic

## Key Features
- Onboarding flow with language and focus area selection
- Voice‑first chat interface powered by Groq and Cartesia APIs
- Guardian safety agent validating every response
- Persistent local storage for progress tracking
- Admin panel for configuration and monitoring

## Success Metrics
- Average session length over 10 minutes
- More than 1,000 daily active users
- At least 20% 7‑day retention
- Positive mood improvement reported by 60% of users
